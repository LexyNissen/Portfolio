About Me

Hey, I'm Lexy! An artist at heart, I grew up in Boulder County Colorado and graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Architecture from the University Of Colorado Denver. I've had an interest in Architecture since childhood and during my time at university, I was able to broaden my horizons and study various forms of Architecture and Design. Though I am excited to work in a range of specialties to diversify my knowledge and skills, I have a major interest in green architecture and sustainable building practices. I am also captivated by gothic and victorian styles as well as classical studies.
