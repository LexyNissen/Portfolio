Live Work Dwell

Combination of living and working spaces with an emphasis on green architecture.

Teams Project

Lexy N, Sarah M, Clairissa E
Feb-May 2022

This live+work project addresses the need for home work spaces after the rise of work from home jobs. It focuses on turning inward and cultivating something precious while commenting on a society inclined toward rapid growth and disregard for the environment. Heavy pollution emitted from industry is creating a growing air quality problem in Denver. Industrial pollutants and toxins also affect artistic materials.

The concept aims to create a shielded space of cultivation for makers whose products may benefit people and the environment. The space favors two makers (a seed banker and a pigment maker) whose work is oriented toward the slow cultivation of plants and responds to multiple environmental issues.
