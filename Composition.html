Concept Study

Spring 2021

CORNFIELD CHASE - INTERSTELLAR

Inspiration for form and design can be found in a multitude of elements. This excercise focused on composition, translation and heirarchy. One fragment from a song was selected to be studied, then the qualities, emotions, and energy of the piece were transcribed into visual physical representations using architectural tools. The resulting linework, done both digitally and by hand should express the atmosphere of the piece.
